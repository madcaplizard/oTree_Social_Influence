Introduction

Welcome to the experiment on the effect of social influence on decision-making.

In this experiment, you will be assigned as either a leader or a follower. Leaders will make their investment decisions first, followed by the followers who will make their decisions after receiving information about what the leaders chose.

The aim of this experiment is to investigate whether participants' decisions are influenced by the decisions of others.

Please carefully read the instructions and make your decisions based on the information provided.
